Welcome to our Add-on of the Learning Analytics Support for the Khan Academy (ALAS-KA) platform.

We have designed and implemented a learning analytics extension that includes new higher level metrics and visualizations. We think these new features will improve the teachers' and students' learning experience in the Khan Academy framework, giving them new intelligent information that is useful for the learning process. Next, there are a few recommendations about the use of ALAS-KA:

  • -Please, read the description of each metric to understand the information you are receiving and how it can be interpreted.
  • -As a teacher you are able to visualize individual information of each of your students in your class; this allows you to keep track of your students in-depth. Also you can visualize global statistics of your entire class as a whole.
